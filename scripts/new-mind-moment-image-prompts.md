# New Mind-Moment Image Prompts

Generation descriptions for the 19 mind moments added to `MIND_EXERCISES` in
`background.js`. They follow the established mind-card visual formula: a faceless,
gender-neutral seated figure (office chair, `walk-and-hydrate` hair) with a single
symbolic thought/speech bubble and lavender-slate motion accents.

Once generated, save each as `illustrations/<slug>.png` (filenames below already
match `stretchSlug()`), then run them through `restyle-prompts.md` for final
line-weight/color QA. Generate larger and resize once to 400x400 with a
transparent background.

## Shared style header (prepend to every prompt)

> Single faceless, gender-neutral figure in clean line-art, seated in a simple
> office chair unless noted. Use the `walk-and-hydrate.png` hairstyle (simple
> androgynous short hair, blank face — no eyes, nose, mouth, or eyebrows). Pure
> outlines only in deep ink-navy `#2A3048`, even rounded strokes matching the line
> weight of `illustrations/restyled/5-4-3-2-1-grounding.png` at 400x400 (~2-3px,
> clearly visible). No fills, shading, gradients, or texture. Use soft
> lavender-slate `#8490A8` ONLY for motion arrows, curves, and accent marks —
> never yellow/orange/peach. Thought and speech bubbles in the same navy outline.
> Transparent background, no border/shadow/glow, centered with generous consistent
> padding. Final output 400x400 PNG.

## Batch 1 — well-being moments

### hand-on-heart.png
Seated figure with both palms resting flat over the center of the chest, shoulders
relaxed and slightly dropped, head gently lowered in a calm, self-soothing posture.
A few short lavender warmth lines radiate softly outward from the hands. No thought
bubble needed.

### where-are-your-feet.png
Seated figure looking downward toward both feet planted flat and evenly on the
floor. Small lavender contact accents (short marks/brackets) at the soles where
they meet the ground, emphasizing grounded weight and connection to the floor.

### a-long-exhale.png
Seated figure, shoulders noticeably relaxed, releasing one long slow breath. A
single long, sweeping lavender exhale curve streams out from near the mouth and
trails far to the side, ending in two or three small fading lavender dots — longer
and calmer than a normal breath line.

### unclench.png
Seated figure consciously softening: shoulders dropping, hands loosening open on
the thighs. Small paired lavender "release" accent marks at the three holding spots
— jaw, shoulders, and hands — suggesting tension easing outward.

### rest-your-eyes-far-away.png
Seated figure with head lifted, gazing into the distance. A long lavender sight-line
extends from the head out to a small lavender target circle far across the frame,
suggesting eyes resting on something far away. Outline only, no iris.

### savor-one-good-thing.png
Seated figure in a relaxed, contented posture. A thought bubble above the head
contains one simple navy-outline sun (or small steaming cup), with a gentle lavender
curved "replay" arrow looping around it to suggest revisiting a good moment.

### send-a-good-thought.png
Seated figure looking warmly outward. A thought bubble releases a small navy-outline
heart that drifts up and away beyond the bubble's edge, trailed by a lavender motion
curve showing it traveling outward toward someone else.

### put-it-down-for-a-minute.png
Seated figure gently lowering a small navy-outline cloud or orb (a "worry") onto a
simple shelf or surface beside the chair, hands releasing it. A downward lavender
motion arrow shows the act of setting it down.

### one-thing-ahead.png
Seated figure looking up and forward with an open posture. A thought bubble holds a
simple horizon line with a small sun rising on it, and a lavender forward arrow
pointing toward it — something small and good ahead.

## Batch 2 — CBT-rooted moments

### thought-or-fact.png
Seated figure considering a thought bubble that contains a small, simple two-pan
balance scale (navy outline). One pan holds a tiny cloud (thought), the other a
small solid square (fact); the scale tips slightly. A small lavender question-mark
accent sits beside the bubble.

### i-m-having-the-thought-that.png
Seated figure leaning back slightly, creating visible distance between the head and
a thought bubble that floats out at arm's length rather than close to the head. A
short string of small lavender dots bridges the gap, emphasizing the space opening
up between the person and the thought.

### name-the-thinking-trap.png
Seated figure calmly observing a thought bubble that contains a single looping,
tangled knot of line (navy outline) — the "thinking trap" pattern. A small lavender
label-tag or pointer marks the knot, suggesting it's being noticed and named, not
fought.

### what-s-most-likely.png
Seated figure facing a thought bubble holding two clouds: one large looming storm
cloud and one much smaller plain cloud beside it. A lavender arrow points away from
the big cloud toward the small one — the most likely outcome, not the scariest.

### a-fairer-thought.png
Seated figure with two small linked thought bubbles. The first holds a jagged,
spiky mark (a harsh thought); a lavender arrow connects to a second bubble holding a
soft rounded mark (a fairer, balanced thought).

### the-next-five-minutes.png
Figure rising up out of the office chair, one foot stepping forward to begin a small
action (behavioral activation — motion, not seated). A small navy-outline
clock/timer in a thought bubble reads about five minutes, with a lavender forward
motion arrow at the stepping foot.

### opposite-action.png
Seated figure at a small fork of two lavender arrows: one faint, curling arrow
pulling back toward withdrawal/curling up, and one clearer arrow taken in the
opposite direction — a small step outward. The figure's posture leans toward the
chosen opposite arrow.

### catch-one-good-fact.png
Seated figure dropping one small navy-outline star into a simple little jar held in
the lap or beside the chair (a positive-data log). A lavender motion line traces the
star falling into the jar, which already holds one or two stars.

### soften-a-should.png
Seated figure with shoulders lifting lighter as a small heavy navy-outline block
(labeled feeling of "should") rises up and away from above the shoulders, carried
off by an upward lavender motion arrow — pressure easing off.

### feelings-aren-t-facts.png
Seated figure calmly watching a large soft cloud drift across a wide thought bubble,
entering one side and beginning to exit the other, with a horizontal lavender motion
trail showing it passing through — present but moving on, not staying.
